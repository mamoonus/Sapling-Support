Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my subscription?

Open the Sapling app and go to Settings > Subscription. You can also manage your subscription through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, and when you reach the paywall screen, tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription will be restored automatically.

How do I clear my data?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Clear Data." This will reset your portfolio and account information.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Sapling uses bank-level encryption and never stores your banking credentials. All connections are read-only.
